💡 Pronunciation Tips

Special French sounds:
• The French 'R' is pronounced in the back of the throat
• 'J' and 'G' make a soft 'zh' sound (like 's' in "treasure")
• 'U' is pronounced with rounded lips (like saying 'ee' with pursed lips)

💡 Number Tips

Key pronunciation notes:
• Numbers ending in 'x' (six, dix) - the 'x' is usually silent
• 'Huit' starts with a silent 'h'
• From 17-19, numbers follow the pattern "ten-seven", "ten-eight", "ten-nine"
• Practice these basics - they're used to build larger numbers!
